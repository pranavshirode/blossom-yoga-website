Our Story

Nurturing wellness through every stage of life

Blossom was built on a simple idea — that yoga should adapt to you, not the other way around.

How Blossom Began

A practice built from real experience

Blossom grew out of more than five years of teaching yoga to women navigating some of the most transformative moments of their lives — early pregnancy, the final weeks before delivery, the tender first months of motherhood, and everyday people simply looking to feel stronger and calmer.

What started as small, in-person sessions has grown into a warm online studio, reaching students wherever they are — on a bedroom mat, in a quiet corner of a living room, or between naps with a newborn nearby. The format changed; the care behind every class never did.

Today, Blossom continues to welcome beginners, working professionals, expecting mothers and new mothers alike, offering a space where every body and every stage is met with patience.

Meet the Instructor

Guided by experience and care

With over five years of dedicated teaching experience across prenatal, postnatal, and general yoga, I've had the privilege of guiding individuals through some of the most transformative stages of their lives.

My approach is rooted in patience, mindfulness, and the belief that yoga is for every body. Whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or looking to deepen your practice, my goal is to create a safe, welcoming space where you can connect with your body and breath.
