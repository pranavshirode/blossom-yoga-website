Prenatal Yoga

Safe, supportive movement for pregnancy

Personalized one-on-one sessions designed around your trimester, energy and any pregnancy-specific concerns. Every pose is adapted for safety, with a strong focus on breathing techniques that carry you through labour.

  • Safe exercises
  • Pelvic strengthening
  • Better breathing
  • Relaxation

Schedule: Personalized one-on-one sessions, flexible timings

Postnatal Yoga

Gentle recovery for new mothers

A restorative path back to strength after delivery. Sessions focus on safe core recovery, posture correction and restoring energy — all at a pace that respects your body's healing timeline.

  • Recovery
  • Core strengthening
  • Posture correction
  • Energy restoration
